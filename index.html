ABOUT

Lookables is a collection of 3333 emotional collectibles living on the Solana Blockchain.
We aim to bring people together through the power of shared tales, desires, memes,
and personal stories through a social hub where everyone can build meaningful
connections, relate with one another and share their experiences with sense of awareness.

LOOKIFESTO

In a technologically complex space consisting of people all over the world, we can all agree that we all
have emotions. Sometimes, we're over-excited over a successful NFT flip or achieving a personal goal outside of NFTs.
Other times we feel down over an argument we had with someone close to us, or perhaps we're burned in an NFT scam.
In and out of the NFT space, we have experiences that ignite emotions within us.
It isn't possible to find someone to express our wins, hopes, joys, and losses with.
Besides, many people who don't trade NFTs cannot relate to the challenges we face.
Our project hopes to change that by offering what some of us may not be experiencing in our daily lives.
A safe space
An open ear
A judgment-free zone
A fun place to vibe out with like-minded people.

What are NFTs?

NFT stands for “Non-Fungible Token” which are unique digital items that are stored
and encrypted on the blockchain that people can buy, own, and trade.
NFTs can simply be digital art but can have various benefits.
They can be treated like membership cards where holders get exclusive access
to things like websites, events, merchandise, and more.
